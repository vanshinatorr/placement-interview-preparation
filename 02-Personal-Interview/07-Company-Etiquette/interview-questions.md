## 1. How Should You Greet the Interviewer?

**Answer:**

I will greet the interviewer politely with a smile and say,

"Good morning, sir/ma'am. It's nice to meet you."

Then I will wait until they ask me to sit.

---

## 2. What Should You Do If You Are Running Late for an Interview?

**Answer:**

I will inform the interviewer or HR as soon as possible, apologize politely, and explain the situation briefly.

I believe informing them in advance shows professionalism.

---

## 3. What Should You Wear for an Interview?

**Answer:**

I will wear clean, formal clothes with polished shoes and make sure my overall appearance is neat and professional.

---

## 4. How Should You Introduce Yourself to the Interviewer?

**Answer:**

I will greet the interviewer, thank them for the opportunity, and then give a short introduction about my education, internship, projects, and career goal.

---

## 5. What Should You Do If You Don't Know an Answer?

**Answer:**

I will honestly say that I don't know the answer instead of guessing.

If possible, I will explain how I would learn or solve it.

---

## 6. Is It Okay to Ask Questions at the End of an Interview?

**Answer:**

Yes.

It shows that I am interested in the role and the company.

I would ask about the team, learning opportunities, or the next steps in the hiring process.

---

## 7. What Should You Do After an Interview?

**Answer:**

I will thank the interviewer for their time and the opportunity.

If appropriate, I will also send a polite thank-you email.

---

## 8. How Should You Behave During an Interview?

**Answer:**

I will stay calm, listen carefully, answer honestly, and maintain a positive attitude.

I will also avoid interrupting the interviewer.

---

## 9. What Should You Do If You Don't Understand a Question?

**Answer:**

I will politely ask the interviewer to repeat or explain the question.

It is better to ask than to answer incorrectly.

---

## 10. Why Is Professional Communication Important?

**Answer:**

Professional communication helps avoid misunderstandings and builds trust.

It also creates a positive impression and improves teamwork.

---

## 11. Should You Speak Negatively About Your Previous Internship or College?

**Answer:**

No.

Even if I had challenges, I would focus on what I learned instead of speaking negatively.

---

## 12. Is It Okay to Admit a Mistake During an Interview?

**Answer:**

Yes.

I believe it is better to be honest, explain what I learned, and show how I improved.

---

## 13. What Should You Do If the Interviewer Interrupts You?

**Answer:**

I will stop speaking, listen carefully, and answer the new question politely.

I will not interrupt them.

---

## 14. How Should You Handle a Stress Interview?

**Answer:**

I will stay calm, answer politely, and not take difficult questions personally.

I will focus on giving honest and logical answers.

---

## 15. Why Is Body Language Important During an Interview?

**Answer:**

Good body language shows confidence and professionalism.

Simple things like maintaining eye contact, sitting properly, and smiling can create a positive impression.

---

## 16. Should You Use Your Phone During an Interview?

**Answer:**

No.

I will keep my phone on silent mode and avoid using it unless the interviewer asks me to.

---

## 17. How Should You End an Interview?

**Answer:**

I will thank the interviewer for their time and say,

"Thank you for the opportunity. It was nice speaking with you. I hope to hear from you soon."

---

## 18. Why Is Punctuality Important?

**Answer:**

Being on time shows respect, responsibility, and professionalism.

It also creates a good first impression.

---

## 19. What Should You Do If You Feel Nervous?

**Answer:**

I will take a deep breath, stay calm, and focus on understanding the question before answering.

I believe confidence improves as the conversation continues.

---

## 20. What Is the Most Important Interview Etiquette?

**Answer:**

I believe the most important etiquette is being honest, respectful, and professional.

Good communication, positive behavior, and a willingness to learn always leave a strong impression.
