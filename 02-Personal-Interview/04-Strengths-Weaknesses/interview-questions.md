# 04 - Strengths & Weaknesses

## Strengths (1–10)

---

# 1. What Is Your Biggest Strength?

**Answer:**

I think my biggest strength is my consistency.

Once I set a goal, I stay committed and work on it regularly. This habit has helped me improve my coding skills, complete my internship successfully, and build projects like ConsistPay.

I believe consistent effort always gives better results over time.

---

# 2. What Are Your Top Three Strengths?

**Answer:**

My top three strengths are consistency, problem-solving, and willingness to learn.

I stay committed to my goals, enjoy solving technical problems, and I am always ready to learn new technologies whenever required.

These qualities have helped me grow throughout my college journey.

---

# 3. Which Strength Has Helped You the Most?

**Answer:**

The strength that has helped me the most is my ability to keep learning.

Whether it was learning new technologies during my internship or building full-stack projects, I always enjoy understanding new concepts and applying them in practice.

I believe this quality will help me throughout my career.

---

# 4. How Has Your Biggest Strength Helped You?

**Answer:**

My consistency has helped me complete long-term goals without giving up.

For example, I stayed regular with DSA practice, completed my internship, and built projects step by step instead of trying to do everything at once.

This habit has improved both my confidence and my technical skills.

# 5. What Strength Would Your Teammates Use to Describe You?

**Answer:**

I think my teammates would describe me as reliable.

If I take responsibility for a task, I try my best to complete it on time.

They would also say that I stay calm during problems and work well with the team.

# 6. Which Technical Strength Are You Most Confident About?

**Answer:**

I am most confident in backend development.

I enjoy building REST APIs, working with databases, and connecting the frontend with the backend.

Through my internship and projects like ConsistPay, I gained practical experience in these areas.

---

# 7. What Personal Quality Makes You Different?

**Answer:**

I think my willingness to learn makes me different.

Whenever I come across something new, I don't hesitate to learn it.

Instead of avoiding challenges, I see them as opportunities to improve myself.

---

# 8. What Is One Strength You Are Most Proud Of?

**Answer:**

The strength I am most proud of is my ability to stay calm while solving problems.

Whenever I face a difficult bug or challenge, I avoid rushing and try to solve it step by step.

This approach has helped me during both my internship and project development.

---

# 9. How Do Your Strengths Help You at Work?

**Answer:**

My strengths help me stay focused, learn quickly, and complete my work responsibly.

I enjoy working with others, take feedback positively, and always try to deliver my best.

These qualities help me contribute effectively to the team.

---

# 10. Which Strength Do You Want to Improve Even More?

**Answer:**

I would like to improve my leadership skills.

I want to become more confident in leading projects, making decisions, and guiding a team.

I believe this will come with experience, and I am always looking for opportunities to improve in this area.


# 11. What Is Your Biggest Weakness?

**Answer:**

One weakness I noticed in myself is that I sometimes start working on a solution too quickly.

Earlier, I focused on coding first, and later I realized that a little planning could have saved time.

Now, I spend a few minutes understanding the problem before I start coding, and it has improved my work.

---

# 12. Tell Me One Weakness You Are Working On

**Answer:**

I used to spend extra time trying to make every small detail perfect.

Over time, I learned that delivering a good solution on time is more important.

Now, I balance quality with deadlines and manage my time better.

---

# 13. What Skill Do You Want to Improve?

**Answer:**

I want to improve my communication skills, especially while explaining technical ideas.

I have already improved by working in teams during my internship and projects, and I continue to practice whenever I get the chance.

---

# 14. What Bad Habit Have You Changed?

**Answer:**

Earlier, I used to hesitate before asking for help when I was stuck.

I thought I should solve every problem on my own.

Now, I first try my best, but if I am still stuck, I ask for guidance at the right time. This helps me learn faster.

---

# 15. Tell Me About a Weakness That Became a Learning Experience

**Answer:**

One weakness was starting implementation without fully understanding the requirements.

Because of this, I sometimes had to make unnecessary changes later.

Now, I first understand the complete requirement, think about the approach, and then start coding.

---

# 16. What Feedback Do You Receive Most Often?

**Answer:**

One feedback I have received is to be more confident while sharing my ideas during discussions.

I have been working on this by participating more in team conversations and explaining my thoughts clearly.

I have seen good improvement over time.

---

# 17. What Do You Find Most Challenging?

**Answer:**

I find it challenging when requirements change in the middle of development.

However, I understand that this is common in software projects.

Instead of getting frustrated, I focus on understanding the new requirements and updating my work accordingly.

---

# 18. What Kind of Work Do You Struggle With?

**Answer:**

I don't struggle with any particular type of work, but I take a little extra time when I work on something completely new.

I first understand the basics, learn the required concepts, and then start the task.

Once I understand it, I become comfortable working on it.

---

# 19. How Do You Overcome Your Weaknesses?

**Answer:**

First, I try to identify where I need to improve.

Then I make small changes in my daily work, take feedback seriously, and practice consistently.

I believe improvement is a continuous process.

---

# 20. What Have You Improved About Yourself in the Last Two Years?

**Answer:**

In the last two years, I have improved both my technical skills and my confidence.

I completed an internship, built full-stack projects, solved more than 300 DSA problems, and became more comfortable working in a team.

These experiences have helped me grow both personally and professionally.
